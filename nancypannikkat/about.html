    Born and raised in Western New York State, Nancy Pannikkat grew up with a love for art, graduating with a Bachelor’s of Fine Arts from the State University of New York, College at Fredonia in 1994. She has resided in the San Francisco Bay Area for almost thirty years with her husband, raising their children while doing non profit work and art as a hobby. Now at age 50 she has revisited her art roots, finding new inspiration and passion to create pieces that translate her love of color, texture, and high contrast in nature. Her many travels bring new subject material whether it be tropics, deserts, mountains, or farmland. Her current collection inspired by the Hawaiian Islands will be on display at The Patterson Library, Octagon Gallery in Westfield, NY February 17th- March 18th 2023.
